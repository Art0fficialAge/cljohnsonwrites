The Book

Elephants and Flowers

Elephants and Flowers is a forthcoming memoir by C.L. Johnson about music, memory, disability, faith, and the long work of becoming visible to oneself.

The book traces a life shaped by sound: childhood rooms, family stories, spiritual hunger, disabled embodiment, and the strange mercy of finding language through another artist’s work. It is not a book about imitation or celebrity worship. It is a memoir about resonance: what happens when music gives form to feelings a person has carried for years without a name.

Expected in 2026, Elephants and Flowers will be available through Amazon KDP in paperback and Kindle formats.

The Author

About C.L. Johnson

C.L. Johnson is a Minnesota writer whose work explores music, memory, disability, faith, grief, and the intimate ways art helps a person survive themselves.

His forthcoming memoir, Elephants and Flowers, traces a life shaped by sound, family memory, spiritual longing, and the slow work of becoming visible inside one’s own body.

He lives in Le Center, Minnesota with his cat, Gary.
