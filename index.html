The Book

Elephants and Flowers

Elephants and Flowers is a memoir about growing up disabled, finding language through music, and coming to understand Prince not only as an artist, but as a companion in the long work of becoming oneself.

It is a story about memory, embodiment, faith, loneliness, beauty, and the private rooms music opens inside a life.

Forthcoming memoir

Author

C.L. Johnson

C.L. Johnson is a writer whose work moves through music, memory, disability, faith, and the slow work of becoming visible inside one’s own body.

He lives in Le Center, Minnesota with his cat, Gary.
